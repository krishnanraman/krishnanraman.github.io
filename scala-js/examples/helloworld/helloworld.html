Knapsack on a radial graph

Rules of the game

  • You are playing against the computer. You = Green, Computer = Red.
  • To begin, pick any pouch. From then on, you can only pick a pouch the previous pouch is connected to.
  • Whoever makes the most money once both of you reach 100 lb wins!
  • If you get stuck, the computer wins! If computer gets stuck, you win!
